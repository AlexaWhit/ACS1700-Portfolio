About Me

My name is Alexa Whitney, and I live in Loveland, Colorado. I am newly married (December 2021) and have three fur-children: Bruno (13 year old Shih Tzu), Ifrit (8 year old Shiba Inu), and Freya (6 month old Persian kitten and the Princess of the family). I was born in Denver, Colorado but have lived in several other states including Wisconsin, Georgia, Florida, and Maryland. My husband and I moved back to Colorado this summer after a year in Maryland/Washington, D.C. and we are so excited to be back in the Rocky Mountains! We enjoy hiking, sampling craft beers/eating at new restaurants, and attending concerts. My personal goal this year is to learn how to roller-skate so that I can join the local roller derby team!

Education

I graduated from Florida State University with a BS in Criminology and after 10 years I went to Colorado State University online and completed my MS in Criminal Justice Administration while working full time. I am now back in school for a third time to obtain my BS in Applied Computer Science through Dominican University of California's online accelerated program.

Criminal Justice

For the last 13 years I have teetered back and forth between probation and the courthouse. Specifically, I was a Probation Officer for misdemeanor DUI and DV cases and was also the Senior Court Clerk and Deputy Court Administrator (Court Manager) for a municipality in Colorado, and then moved to Washington,D.C. where I was the Courtroom Deputy for the Honorable Judge Florence Y. Pan with the United States District Court for the District of Columbia. I worked one block away from the Capital building, so you can imagine how beautiful and special my walk to and from the train station was! I worked on several interesting cases, including this high-profile antitrust case against two big publishing houses where Stephen King testified at trial (learn more about the trial here).
